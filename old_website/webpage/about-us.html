About us

Gnarly Grey is a microelectronic architecture company focused, but not limited to capabilities surrounding imaging and graphics solutions. Gnarly Grey has over 15 years experience in FPGA and ASIC prototyping, IC device interfacing, signal processing and digital design architectures.

Our biggest focused is ‘on-time’ development and manufacturing, quick turns and iterative cost reduction methods for our customers. We believe in always delivering on-time and improving through incremental revisions. Over many projects we have seen this method succeed over spending tireless hours and budget on first revision perfection. We complement this with a pool of international engineers and balance budget and time respectively to create the most appropriate fit for our customers.
